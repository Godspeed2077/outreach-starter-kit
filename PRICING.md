# Pricing

Outreach Starter Kit is a paid subscription product.

## Subscription

**$5.00 USD per month** for the founding 100 subscribers. Price escalates by $5/mo for each subsequent 100 active subscribers (next cohort $10/mo, then $15/mo, etc).

Founding-100 subscribers are **locked at their original price for life** as long as their subscription stays active. Cancellation forfeits the lifetime lock.

## What you get

- Access to the private GitHub repo containing the full kit.
- All future updates (new skills, new send tiers, new channel clients) while subscribed.
- The right to use and modify the kit locally for your own outreach operations.

## What you don't get

- Redistribution rights. The license forbids forking to public repos, reselling, or sharing access with non-subscribers.
- Support guarantees. Best-effort email/issues support, no SLA.
- Refunds. See below.

## No refunds

**Subscription payments are non-refundable.** Once you have been granted access to the private repo, no portion of any payment will be refunded for any reason — including cancellation, dissatisfaction, or lack of use.

The reason: once you have access to the code, you have it. Even if your subscription is cancelled and access is revoked, you have already seen the implementation. We cannot un-show the code, so we do not offer refunds.

If this policy is unacceptable, do not subscribe.

## How onboarding works

1. Pay via the Stripe payment link above.
2. After payment, you will receive an email from Stripe with a confirmation.
3. Reply to that email (or send a separate message to randyrockwell05@gmail.com) with your **GitHub username**.
4. Within 24 hours (usually faster), you will receive a GitHub invitation to collaborate on the private repository.
5. Accept the invitation, then clone the repo: `gh repo clone Godspeed2077/outreach-starter-kit`
6. Follow the README setup instructions.

## How cancellation works

1. Cancel through the Stripe customer portal link in your original receipt email, or email randyrockwell05@gmail.com.
2. Your access to the private repo will be revoked at the end of the current billing period.
3. Per the license, you must delete all local copies of the kit upon access revocation.

## Founding-price lock terms

If you subscribe while the current price is $X, you are locked at $X for life as long as your subscription remains continuously active. **Cancellation forfeits the lock**, and re-subscription is at the then-current price.

The price will be raised by $5 each time the active subscriber count crosses a new 100-subscriber threshold (100, 200, 300, etc).
